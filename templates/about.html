About the Design Toolbox

Our engineering toolbox is a comprehensive platform designed to support mechanical engineers and designers in the preliminary design of key vehicle components. With a focus on fundamental engineering principles, we provide accessible computational tools for a range of automotive systems.

Our Mission

To empower engineers and enthusiasts with user-friendly design tools that simplify complex mechanical calculations, accelerate the initial design phase, and foster a deeper understanding of component engineering.

Parametric Design

Quickly calculate key component dimensions based on fundamental engineering parameters.

Component-Level Focus

Explore detailed design calculations for individual parts, from brake systems to engine components.

Rapid Prototyping

Use our tools to rapidly iterate on initial designs and explore different engineering trade-offs.

Key Design Capabilities

  • Brake Systems: Design and analyze disc, caliper, and brake pad parameters.
  • Tyre Design: Calculate tyre dimensions and contact patch characteristics.
  • Engine Components: Perform preliminary design calculations for pistons and connecting rods.
  • Stress & Force Analysis: Simplified calculations for understanding component loads.
  • Geometric Sizing: Determine critical dimensions for mechanical parts.

Our toolbox is continuously updated to incorporate new design modules and engineering methodologies, ensuring access to relevant and practical design resources.
